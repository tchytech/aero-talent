About Us

Meet The CEO

Captain Horace Millar-Jaja, a British-born Nigerian airline pilot with an impressive 40-year aviation career. After training at the prestigious Oxford Air Training School in the UK and earning a degree in Applied Social Sciences, he took flight.

Horace's exceptional aviation career commenced in 1983 with Imani Aviation Co Ltd then to Nigerian Airways in 1985 followed by a tenure as a First Officer with Air Foyle/TNT International Luton Uk. He took his Command with Okada Air.Served as Chief Pilot at Bellview Airlines and earned wide-bodied aircraft experience on the Airbus 300-600R.In 2000 Horace took flight to Vietnam Airlines as a Captain residing in Ho Chi Minh City for a year before returning to West Africa due to 9/11 where he joined Ghana. Airways flying the DC9-51 .Africa One in Uganda recognized his expertise, appointing him Chief Pilot: Training/Captain till 2003.

Our Story

In the dynamic world of aviation, finding the right talent can be the key to soaring success. This is where Aero-Talent steps in, offering a unique blend of expertise and innovation to match the aviation industry's ever-evolving needs.

Aero-Talent doesn't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. We recognize that each aviation organization is unique, whether it's a major airline, a charter service, or a cargo carrier. That's why we tailor our talent search to match your specific needs, be it aircraft type, route specialization, or operational preferences.

Our Mission

At Aero Talent, we're on a mission to bridge the gap between aviation companies in need of skilled pilots and aviation professionals who are eager to explore new horizons.

Our vision is to create a seamless and efficient process that connects the perfect pilots with the right positions, all while upholding the utmost standards of professionalism and expertise.

We understand that every pilot brings a unique set of skills and experiences to the cockpit. That's why our approach is personalized – we take into account your expertise, preferences, and aspirations to ensure a match that propels your career to new heights.
